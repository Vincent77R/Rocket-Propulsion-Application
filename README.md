# Rocket-Propulsion-Application
Solve the following with a MATLAB script.
The equation of motion of a rocket-propelled sled is, from Newton's law,

m dv/dt = f - cv 

When m is the sled mass , f is the rocket thrust, and c is an air resistance coefficient. Suppose that m = 1000 kg and c = 500N s/m. Suppose also that v(0)=0
• Plot the velocity of the rocket for the first 10 seconds for four values of f:
20,000, 40,000, 60,000, and 80,000 N.
o Include a title, axis labels, a grid, and a legend
m dv/dt = f - cv 
The velocity will be in m/s.
